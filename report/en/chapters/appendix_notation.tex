\section{Notation Table}\label{sec:notation}

The table below mirrors the macros defined in
\code{shared/notation.tex}. Every symbol used in the body must
appear here; a static check (\texttt{check\_notation.py}) verifies
that no body symbol is undefined.

\begin{tabularx}{\columnwidth}{@{}lX@{}}
  \toprule
  Symbol & Meaning \\
  \midrule
  $\Nodes$            & set of nodes in the search tree \\
  $\Tree$             & search tree $(\Nodes, E)$ \\
  $n\in\Nodes$        & individual node \\
  $\children{n}$      & children of $n$ \\
  $\parentof{n}$      & parent of $n$ \\
  $\depth{n}$         & depth of $n$ \\
  $\statefn{n}$       & node state $\in\{\StateOpen,\StateEval,\StateDone,\StateFailed\}$ \\
  $\nodescore{n}$     & scalar reward of $n$ \\
  $\axes{n}$          & per-axis evaluation vector \\
  $\aggregate$        & axis $\to$ scalar aggregator \\
  $\mathrm{div}(n)$   & diversity bonus (\cref{eq:diversity}) \\
  $\_\mathrm{fallback\_score}(n)$ & deterministic fallback ranking (\cref{eq:fallback}) \\
  $\Tmax,\Cmax$       & step / cost budgets \\
  $\Rubric$           & rubric tree \\
  $\rubricitem{i}$    & rubric leaf $i$'s category \\
  $\rubricweight{i}$  & rubric leaf $i$'s weight \\
  $\judgefn{i}$       & rubric leaf $i$'s judge \\
  $\paper$            & paper draft \\
  $\paperscore{\paper}$ & paper aggregate score (\cref{eq:paperscore}) \\
  $\refine$           & refine operator (\cref{eq:refine}) \\
  $\ckpt$             & checkpoint directory \\
  $\lineage{n}$       & lineage chain ending at $n$ \\
  \bottomrule
\end{tabularx}
